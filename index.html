The Role of Shipping in Colonial New England

An Interactive Exploration of History

Welcome to colonialatlantic.com, where you can learn history by doing it. This website is designed to be interactive, leading the user on their own course to find the answers to two key questions: how did shipping affect our nation's social roots, and how did it affect our economy. Perhaps the best use of knowledge of history is to help you understand current events, so after learning the answers to these questions, you will hopefully have a historical basis for analysis of current exploration of new frontiers, such as the potential exploration and colonization of space that seems to loom in the near future.

On this website, you will find interactive timelines, maps, and graphs that aim to answer the key questions. At the bottom of the page, you will find a completion tracker, which allows you to know when you have completed all of the activities. When the tracker bar fills up completely, you will gain access to the final conclusions of the text. As you explore this website, keep in mind how the lessons of history affect our modern-day world. Remember to have fun!
